How It Works

We employ a structured 4-stage framework, allowing your team to build confidence and clarity at each step:

1. Gap Analysis: Thorough review of current infrastructure, documentation, and practices against accreditation norms.

2. SOP Development: We help you create customized Standard Operating Procedures that reflect real workflows while meeting regulatory requirements.

3. Staff Training: Department-specific orientation and skill sessions to embed compliance culture across all levels.

4. Mock Audit: A full internal audit that mimics the real inspection, with clear action points and corrective pathways.
