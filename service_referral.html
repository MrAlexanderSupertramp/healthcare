How It Works

Our process is designed to reduce confusion, build clarity, and give every patient the confidence to proceed. Whether you're calling for an urgent cardiac case or just looking for a dialysis center within budget — we bring logic, care, and speed together.

Here's how we work:

  1. Initial Contact: You or a caregiver contacts us via phone, WhatsApp, or a form on our website.
  2. Understanding the Case: We gather relevant information — illness, budget constraints, location, urgency level, medical reports if available.
  3. Research & Evaluation: Our experts review hospital options across your region, filtering based on medical fit, ethical standards, affordability, and government tie-ups.
  4. Referral Suggestions: We send you 2–3 well-matched hospital options with reasoning, expected admission flow, and cost guidelines.
  5. Onboarding Support: Once you choose, we help with the next steps — scheduling visits, understanding government scheme eligibility, or navigating documentation.

This entire process is managed by real people — not bots — trained in both clinical logic and empathetic conversation.
